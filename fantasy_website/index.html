The Seven Skies

Let me tell you, about the world called The Seven Skies, a world where millions enter with one goal in mind- to conquer the seven parallel worlds and to gain the title of The Sky King. The world of The Seven Skies consists of seven parallel worlds for players to explore and conquer, each one themed with a different sky- Rainy, Clear, Sunny, Cloudy, Snowy, Day and Night. Each world is different with their own towns and cities, items, and monsters that you’ll only find there, although there might be some monsters you can find in two or more worlds.

Each world has different monsters of all kinds to battle against and defeat, each one themed to the one of the seven parallel worlds- to rise through ranks by defeating them. Every world has a secret boss room that you, yourself will have to find. Only those who defeat the boss will have the authorization to go through the door to the next parallel world and gain a special item of that class! Meaning, if you are a Magician then you could gain some type of magic item or if you are a Knight, you could gain a powerful, (maybe even rare) sword or shield. But those who fail will start over in the Beginning Town of that world.

New weapons and items can be unlocked the farther you go through each world. The farther you get and the higher your level goes, the more powerful you become. You may seek out and challenge other players to duels in order to gain experience and level up. The winner will receive a prize of money of difference amounts, depending on the level that player is on. The loser will either end up that world’s Beginning Town, ending up with nothing, or can be revived and join that players team if wanted. In this world, you can choose from six different classes- The Fighter Class, The Magician Class, The Rogue Class, The Cleric Class, The Ranger Class, and Rarer Class Archetypes; however, with each class there are different sized subgroups to choose from. But there is one thing you must know, you get to choose what type of fighter or wizard you can be, but the system chooses the Class you are in.

Δ The Fighter Class

The tank (power) of the group and most basic and broad class. The Fighter is basically, the strong guy with heavy armor, a large melee weapon, and possibly a shield or secondary weapon. Compared to the other classes, Fighters tend to be powerful but have slower speed than the rest. From the subground of this Class is but not limited too:

  • Barbarian
  • Knight
  • Swashbuckler
  • Warlord
  • Paladin

θ The Magician Class

It has a variety of names- Mages, Wizards, Sorcerers, Witches, Warlocks, etc. They have many varieties of functional magic and groups to choose from but are the rarest of all of the classes. Some of them uses wands or staffs to channel their power through while others use hand movements to make their power work. These players tend to be hunted because of the vast power they can get with each level they go up. From the subgroup of this Class is but not limited to:

  • Necromantic Magician
  • Summoner Magician
  • Nature Magician
  • Illusionist Magician

Γ The Rogue Class

Usually dexterous thieves or treasure-hunters who experts in stealth, infiltration, lock picking, traps and the disarming thereof, sneak attacks, and attacking from the rear. They usually specialize with light blades and daggers and tend to be quick but fragile, limited to light armor, but deal a lot of damage when allowed to do so. From the subgroup of this Class is but not limited to:

  • Thief
  • Assassin
  • Ninja
  • Scout

Δ The Cleric Class

The Medic or some variation on dedicated healer and usually draws their powers from some type of god that some can choose while others cannot. Their magic generally requires them to stick to a certain doctrine to access it, but usually comes with less of a price or chance of backfiring. Clerics often focus on healing and party buffs, but sometimes they are offensively useful against "unholy" enemies such as demons and the undead. From the subgroup of this Class is but not limited to:

  • Priest
  • Battle Priest
  • Witch Doctor

Ψ The Ranger Class

A woodsman skilled at surviving in the wild. Archery is generally their favored skill, although most can fall back on swordplay if necessary. Rangers may also be skilled in some form of wilderness or nature magic. They may be very good at fighting a specific type of enemy, and often take on the role of The Hunter against such foes. Rarely, a Ranger may have access to guns as well as bows. From the subgroup of this Class is but not limited to:

  • Sniper Ranger
  • Bow and Blade Ranger
  • Beastmaster Ranger
  • Magical Ranger

Ω Rarer Class Archetypes

It is class that range with lesser known classes then the other 6 classes. They are typical ones who run certain stores or events for the game as well as the Black Market. From the subgroup of this Class is but not limited to:

  1. Alchemist
  2. Psychic
  3. Shop Keeper with Black Market Dealer on the side
  4. Monk
  5. Engineer
  6. Bard with Dancer on the side

A well-balanced party of these players will usually benefit from it. May it be good or bad, you’ll just have to see that when the time comes. However, if players have a hard time forming a party, you may join a guild that will usually accepts any player no matter what class or group they are in. Although, you should do well to remember that each guild is different and has its own standards. Every player will have a second name or username from their real one in the game of their choosing and cannot have the same one as another player. If players die, they will either start over in the world's Beginning Town or have the option to let a party member (if they have one) revive them with an item.

Collect items for selling, fighting, healing; even cooking! For so many things you are able to do! Discover new weapons that can increase your power and defense. Rise up through the different levels of the 7 parallel worlds and make new friends throughout the entire gameplay.


But remember most of all, have fun, after all, this is The Seven Skies.
